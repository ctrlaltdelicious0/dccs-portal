About Us!

Welcome to Don Carlo Cavina School, a place where faith, education, and community come together to shape the leaders of tomorrow. As a Catholic institution, we are dedicated to fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and spiritual development in our students from Kindergarten through to our College of Business.

At Don Carlo Cavina School, we believe in providing a holistic educational experience. Our K-12 program is designed to nurture the intellectual, emotional, and moral development of each student, preparing them for success in both their academic and personal lives. Philosophy

Don Carlo Cavina School, Inc, a Catholic institution inspired by the Salesian Spirituality, believes that the Cavinians are the hope of tomorrow who are globally competent, educated in the love and holy fear of God and makes the Gospel of Christ the criterion and basis of life.

Mission

Don Carlo Cavina School learners are well-rounded Christians and productive citizens who live in service to God and others.

Vision

Don Carlo Cavina School, Inc. is a learning institution animated by the Love of God that instills Christian values and promotes academic excellence in response to the needs of the Church and the common good of the society.
