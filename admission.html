Basic Education

New Student Applicants and Student Transferees

Step 1: Secure and accomplish an application form for Admission Test from the Registrar's Office.

• Pre-School (Yellow Form)

• Grade School (Blue Form)

• Junior High School (Green Form)

• Senior High School (Pink Form)

Step 2: Submit the application form together with the following requirements to the Registrar's Office.

• Certified photocopy of latest Report Card (Form 138)

• Two (2) copies of 1x1 ID colored picture

• Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate

• Photocopy of NSO Birth Certificate

• Certification of Good Moral Character

• Recommendation Form from the Guidance Counselor (Secure form from the Guidance Counselor's Office)

Note: Submit additional requirement for enrollment:

• Original Report Card (Form 138) with complete grades

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS (For Foreign Students Only)

• Photocopy of Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR)

• Study Permit approved by the Department of Education (Dep Ed)

Step 3: Student's Applicant will be scheduled for an interview and pay the Admission Test Fee at the Accounting Office.

Step 4: Secure the date of Admission Test from the Guidance Office.

Step 5: Student applicants who passed the entrance test and interview are required to submit the additional requirement for enrollment.

• Original Report Card (Form 138) with complete grades

Step 6: Follow the enrollment procedures below!

Enrollment Procedures

Step 1: Present the Report Card (Form 138) upon enrollment to the Admission Office.

Step 2: Secure the Registration Form (White Form) from the Admission Office and accomplishes it well.

Step 3: Submit the accomplished registration form to the Registrar's Office for registering, encoding and updating of student's information.

Step 4: Proceed to the School Clinic for medical update and interview.

Step 5: Proceed to the Accounting Office for the assessment of fees and payment.

Step 6: Proceed to the Computer Laboratory for ID picture taking.

Tertiary Education

New Student Applicants and Student Transferees

Step 1: Secure the DCCS Application Form for Admission from the Admission's Office and accomplishes it well.

Step 2: Submit the DCCS Application Form for Admission together with the following requirements to the Registrar's Office:

• Certified photocopy of latest Report Card (Form 138)

• Four (4)copies of 2x2 ID colored pictures and student's name written at the back

• Two (2) copies of 1x1 ID colored pictures and student's name written at the back

• 1 Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate

• 2 Photocopies of NSO Birth Certificate

• Original Certificate of Good Moral Character

• 2 Photocopies of NCAE Result

Step 3: Take the DCCS EntranceTest (DCET). The student applicants may bring two (2) pencils with eraser.

Step 4: Test result will be released by the Guidance Office.

Step 5: Student applicants who passed the DCCS Entrance Test (DCET) will be notified about the specified date and time for their interview with the Dean.

Step 6: After the interview, student applicants are required to submit their additional pertinent documents to the Registrar's Office, to wit:

New Student Applicants

• Original High School Report Card (Form 138) with complete grades and with eligibility certificate indicated and signed by the authorized school official of the previous school.

• Certification of honors received duly signed by the High School Principal for Valedictorians and Salutatorians

Students' Transferees

• Transfer Credentials / Honorable Dismissal

• for applicants of BS in Accountancy, the student must have an average of at least 85%.

• for applicants of BS in Accounting Technology, the student must have an average of at least 80%.

Step 7: Follow the enrollment procedures below!

Enrollment Procedures

Step 1: Present the Report Card (Form 138) upon enrollment to the Admission Office.

Step 2: Secure the Registration Form (White Form) from the Admission Office and accomplishes it well.

Step 3: Submit the accomplished registration form to the Registrar's Office for registering, encoding and updating of student's information.

Step 4: Proceed to the School Clinic for medical update and interview.

Step 5: Proceed to the Accounting Office for the assessment of fees and payment.

Step 6: Proceed to the Computer Laboratory for ID picture taking.
