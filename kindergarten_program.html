Kindergarten Program

Our Kindergarten program at Don Carlo Cavina School provides a nurturing and engaging environment where young learners develop foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and socialization. Through play-based activities and interactive learning, we focus on fostering creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning that sets the stage for their educational journey.
