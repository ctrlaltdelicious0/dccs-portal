Academic Programs

At Don Carlo Cavina School, we offer the K-12 Program, which includes Kindergarten and 12 years of basic education. This program consists of six years of primary education, four years of Junior High School, and two years of Senior High School. It is designed to provide ample time for students to master key concepts and skills, foster lifelong learning, and prepare graduates for higher education, vocational training, employment, and entrepreneurship.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program at Don Carlo Cavina School provides a nurturing and engaging environment where young learners develop foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and socialization. Through play-based activities and interactive learning, we focus on fostering creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning that sets the stage for their educational journey.

Primary Education

Our Primary Education program at Don Carlo Cavina School focuses on building strong academic foundations in core subjects like math, language, science, and social studies. We emphasize the development of critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills while fostering curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Junior High School

Our Junior High School program at Don Carlo Cavina School offers a well-rounded curriculum that encourages academic growth, personal development, and critical thinking. Students explore a variety of subjects, including mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies, while also developing important life skills such as responsibility, teamwork, and leadership in a supportive and engaging environment.

Senior High School

Our Senior High School program at Don Carlo Cavina School provides specialized tracks in academic, technical-vocational, and sports programs to prepare students for higher education, career pathways, and entrepreneurship. We offer strands under these tracks including STEM, ABM, HUMSS, and ICT-TVL. Through a rigorous curriculum, hands-on learning experiences, and personal development, we equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the next chapter of their lives.
